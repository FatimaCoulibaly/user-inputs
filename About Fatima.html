Hi, I'm Fatima Coulibaly.

My Story

From a young age I've had an interest in visual arts. The stories that images could tell and the methods that an artist used to tell them intrigues me. I enjoy analyzing the meaning behind images and films and discussings the ways in which an artist conveys the messages. This has led me to pursue a bachelor degree in Culture & Media at Eugene Lang College of Liberal Arts at The New School. Media is important to me because of the way people use it to interact within society. I'm curious about the ways people use different forms of media in the past, present, and future.

Additionally, I'm interested in learning about different cultures. I'm based in New York but in the future I would love to travel and work in other cities around the world. Rio de Janeiro is at the top of my list. I have another website with a focus on learning about Brazilian culture. Feel free to check it out here.

My Experiences

Currently I work as a projects intern for the nonprofit art organization CITYarts, Inc.

Most of my work experience has been in the administrative area. Generally, I complete tasks relating to research, outreach to the public, and data organization. When I'm not working, I enjoy improving my skills in web development. HTML & CSS are the primary language markups that I'm familar with.
